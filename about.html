About Events and Tours Organization

Events and Tours Organization is an annual event organized by the team of our company. It is a platform for artists to showcase their talents and creativity.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a platform for Lesotho artist to showcase their talents and creativity. We believe that everyone has something unique to offer and we want to provide an opportunity for students to express themselves.

Our Team

We are a team of passionate students who are dedicated to organizing a successful college festival. Our team consists of students from various departments who bring their own unique perspectives and ideas to the table.
